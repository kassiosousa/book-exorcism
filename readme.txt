
Game developed for Global Game Jam 2016

"Book Exorcism", developed by Kassio Sousa and Hedvan Pinto. The game is a point & click where an exorcist priest and his apprentice go to a library to study exorcism rituals. Something goes wrong and the priest is possessed. Now, you as an apprentice, need to consult the books and find out what to do and find the secret word to exorcise the demon.
